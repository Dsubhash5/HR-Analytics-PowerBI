# HR Analytics and Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This project looks at HR workforce data using Power BI to find out what is happening with employees how the workforce is spread out and why people are leaving. The dashboard gives us information to help the HR team make good decisions and come up with ways to keep employees from leaving.

## Tools Used

* Power BI

* DAX

* Excel

* Data Analysis

* Data Visualization

## Dataset

* Total Employees: 12,593

* Active Employees: 10,367

* Terminated Employees: 2,226

* Attrition Rate: 17.68%

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Workforce Overview

* Total Employees

* Percentage of employees at Headquarters

* Percentage of employees who work from home

* Average Age of employees

* Hiring Trend

* How many men and women work here

* Which departments people work in

* What race people are

* Where people are from

### 2. Attrition Analysis

* Attrition Rate

* How many employees left

* How many employees are still here

* How long people usually work here

* Why people leave departments

* When people were. Why they left

* Where people are from and why they leave

* What job titles people had when they left

## Key Insights

* The rate at which employees are leaving is higher than what's normal in the industry.

* Most employees are from Ohio.

* A big part of the workforce is in Engineering.

* The Marketing team does not have people compared to Engineering.

* The workforce has the same number of men and women.

## Business Recommendations

* We should try to keep employees from leaving by starting programs to keep them.

* We should let more people work from home.

* We should hire people for the Marketing team.

* We should not have many employees in one place.

* We should make sure we have a plan, for when people leave their jobs.
